Incident Date: 17 June 2026

Location: Hyderabad International Logistics Warehouse Shamshabad,

  -------------------
  WITNESS STATEMENT
  -------------------

My name is Sarah Connor. I work as a night security supervisor at the
Hyderabad International Logistics Warehouse.

At approximately 23:30, I noticed a red Ford Mustang (Registration:
TS09AB4587) entering through Gate 3. The vehicle was driven by Rahul
Verma. He was accompanied by another individual named Imran Khan.

Although deliveries normally stop by 22:00, they claimed they had
authorization from ShadowCorp Logistics Pvt. Ltd. I could not verify the
authorization because our internal server was offline.

Around 23:42, I observed Rahul unloading two large black metal
containers from the vehicle. The containers were marked “Industrial
Equipment”, but they appeared unusually heavy.

Minutes later, I heard a loud metallic sound from Warehouse Section B.

When I reached the area, I saw warehouse manager Ajay Mehta arguing with
Rahul Verma.

Ajay repeatedly shouted: “You were never supposed to bring the drives
here.”

Immediately afterward, Imran Khan picked up a crowbar lying near the
loading dock.

During the investigation, police recovered: - One blood-stained
crowbar - Three encrypted SSD drives - One damaged surveillance DVR -
Multiple fake shipment labels

The surveillance cameras covering Section B stopped recording at exactly
23:39.

Our IT department later confirmed that someone remotely disabled the
recording system.

Financial investigators also discovered that approximately ₹48,50,000
had been transferred from ShadowCorp Logistics Pvt. Ltd. to a shell
company called Blue Horizon Exports exactly six hours before the
incident.

Bank records indicate that Rahul Verma was listed as an authorized
signatory for Blue Horizon Exports.

Vehicle GPS logs show that the Ford Mustang remained near the warehouse
until 23:58 before traveling toward the Outer Ring Road.

Police recovered fingerprints belonging to Rahul Verma on one of the
encrypted SSD drives.

  --------------------------
  SECOND WITNESS STATEMENT
  --------------------------

My name is Priya Nair.

I operate the fuel station opposite the warehouse.

At approximately 23:20, I observed the same red Ford Mustang arrive.

However, I clearly remember only one person exiting the vehicle.

I did not see Imran Khan at that time.

The driver purchased fuel using a corporate payment card issued to
ShadowCorp Logistics.
