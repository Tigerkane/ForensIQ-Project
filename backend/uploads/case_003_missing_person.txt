========================================================= FORENSIQ
INVESTIGATION FILE Case ID: FIQ-2026-093 Classification: Confidential
=========================================================

Incident Date: Disappearance reported 5 May 2026 (last confirmed
contact 4 May 2026, approximately 21:00)

Location: Lakeview Residency, Gachibowli, Hyderabad (residence) and
Hi-Tech Trade Towers, Madhapur (last confirmed workplace location)

Missing Person: Mr. Anand Bhatt, age 34, Senior Project Manager,
Nimbus Infraworks Pvt. Ltd.

  -------------------
  WITNESS STATEMENT
  -------------------

My name is Kavita Bhatt. I am the wife of the missing person, Anand
Bhatt.

On the evening of 4 May, Anand told me he was going to meet a
colleague to "settle something" before a project deadline review the
next morning. He left our flat at approximately 20:30.

He did not say who he was meeting, but he seemed tense. He had been
arguing with two people from his office over the past two weeks —
his deputy, Mr. Sanjay Rathi, and a junior associate, Ms. Neha Desai.

I tried calling him at 22:40 and again at 23:15. Both calls went
unanswered. His phone has been switched off since approximately 21:50,
according to the telecom provider.

I filed a missing person report the next morning, 5 May, at 09:00,
after he did not return home and did not appear for the 8:30 review
meeting.

  --------------------------
  SECOND WITNESS STATEMENT
  --------------------------

My name is Sanjay Rathi. I am the deputy project manager and reported
directly to Mr. Bhatt.

I admit Anand and I had a disagreement two weeks ago over budget
allocations on the Riverside Towers project. He believed I had
inflated vendor invoices. I told him this was a misunderstanding and
that I would clarify it with documentation.

On 4 May, I was at Hi-Tech Trade Towers until approximately 20:45,
finishing a report. I left through the main entrance, which security
can confirm via the building's swipe-card log.

I did not see Anand that evening after he left the office around
18:30. I went home and did not leave again that night.

I am aware that Anand was also unhappy with Ms. Desai over a separate
matter, though I do not know the details.

  ---------------------------
  THIRD WITNESS STATEMENT
  ---------------------------

My name is Neha Desai. I am a junior associate on the Riverside Towers
project.

Anand had threatened, about ten days ago, to report me to HR for
submitting falsified site inspection reports. I disputed this, but he
said he had proof and intended to act on it after the deadline review.

I left the office at 19:15 on 4 May and went directly to a yoga class
that runs from 19:45 to 21:00 at a studio four kilometers from the
office. The instructor and several other attendees can confirm I was
present for the full session.

After class, I went home. I did not see or speak to Anand that
evening.

  ---------------------
  INVESTIGATION NOTES
  ---------------------

Known Individuals - Anand Bhatt (Missing Person) - Kavita Bhatt
(Spouse) - Sanjay Rathi (Deputy Project Manager) - Neha Desai (Junior
Associate) - Building Security Staff, Hi-Tech Trade Towers - Yoga
Studio Staff and Attendees (corroborating witnesses for Ms. Desai)

Organizations - Nimbus Infraworks Pvt. Ltd. - Riverside Towers Project
(under internal audit review)

Vehicle Anand Bhatt's car, a silver Hyundai Verna (Registration:
TS07CD2291), was found abandoned in the parking lot of a closed
shopping complex on the outskirts of Gachibowli, approximately 6 km
from his residence, at 11:40 on 5 May. Keys were still in the
ignition. No signs of forced entry or struggle were found inside the
vehicle.

Evidence - Anand's phone, switched off since 21:50 on 4 May, last cell
tower ping placed it near the Hi-Tech Trade Towers area - A folder of
documents recovered from Anand's office desk, containing draft notes
alleging discrepancies in both vendor invoices (linked to Mr. Rathi)
and site inspection reports (linked to Ms. Desai) - Building swipe-card
log confirming Mr. Rathi's exit at 20:47 - Yoga studio attendance sheet
confirming Ms. Desai's presence from 19:45 to 21:00, signed by the
instructor - A torn page from a notebook found in Anand's car,
containing a partial, unfinished sentence: "I know what both of you—"
- No CCTV coverage at the shopping complex parking lot; cameras were
removed during ongoing renovation

Financial Activity No unusual financial activity has been found in
Anand Bhatt's personal accounts. An internal audit of Riverside Towers
project finances, requested by Anand two weeks prior to his
disappearance, was scheduled to begin on 6 May but has been
suspended pending the outcome of this investigation.

Possible Contradiction Mr. Rathi's swipe-card log places him leaving
the building at 20:47, which would make it difficult for him to have
intercepted Anand, who left earlier at 18:30 according to Rathi's own
statement but did not leave his residence until 20:30 according to his
wife. The discrepancy between Anand's office departure time and his
home departure time has not been explained by any witness.

Ms. Desai's alibi is corroborated by multiple attendees, but the yoga
studio is located on the route between Anand's residence and the
shopping complex where his car was found, raising the question of
whether she could have been involved before or after her class.

Unresolved Question The torn notebook page suggests Anand intended to
confront two people, not one, on the night of his disappearance. Both
Mr. Rathi and Ms. Desai had documented motives and were named in
Anand's draft notes. Neither has a witness who can account for their
whereabouts at the precise moment Anand's car was likely left at the
shopping complex, estimated between 21:30 and 23:00.
